Hi! I'm Joshua, a robotics research engineer based in Los Angeles, CA. I am currently a member of the Autonomous Robotics and Control Lab at Caltech, developing the next generation of vehicles and algorithms for earth and space applications.

I earned my B.S. and M.S. in Mechanical Engineering at Northwestern University.

I have a background in mechatronics, embedded systems, control systems, manufacturing, and rapid prototyping. I look forward to applying my skills in various applications of robotics to foster creativity and innovation, advance technologies, and transform the relationship between humans and robots.
