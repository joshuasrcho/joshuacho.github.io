I am an aspiring robotics engineer with an interest in mobile robotic systems and path planning. I currently attend Northwestern University, and plan to graduate in winter 2022 with both a Bachelor's and a Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering through Northwestern's combined BS/MS program. I am a laboratory assistant at Northwestern's Center for Robotics and Biosystems, where I assist robot assembly and coursework design for graduate studies in Robot Operating Systems and computer vision.

I have a background in mechatronics, embedded systems, feedback control, manufacturing, and rapid prototyping through working on various projects inside and outside of the classrooms, as well as in professional settings. I look forward to applying my skills in various applications of robotics to foster creativity and innovation, advance technologies, and transform the relationship between humans and robots.

When I'm not working on robots or tinkering with electronics, I enjoy hiking, watching soccer, playing video games with friends, and trying out new pasta recipes.
